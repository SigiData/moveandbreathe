Decision rule
The app starts from 100 and subtracts penalties. Air quality can remove up to 60 points. Comfort factors adjust the rest.
WHO alignment
Pollutants are compared against WHO reference values for PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, O₃, CO, and SO₂.
Data update cycle
Environmental data refreshes automatically every 2 hours between 05:00 and 21:00.
